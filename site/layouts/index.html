Features

Masternodes

Masternodes play a vital role in the blockchain by governing few activities of the network, Masternodes get 50% of every block reward

InstantX

InstantX can be used for near instant payments . Masternodes confirms the InstantX transactions

Governance

Decision making is done by the network for new implementations and changes with the help of Masternodes through Individual voting

Decentralized

There is no central authority, all the transactions are carried out and monitored by the peer-peer network itself

Faster Transactions

The Scrypt algorithm enables almost instant and free transactions however it takes a little time for confirming the transactions

Active Development

Our Team is constantly working to provide more features for swift and better transactions

GPU Mineable

Scrypt algorithm enables mining on an average PC right from your wallet or third party mining softwares

E-commerce Ready

Merchant and user friendly, accept Philscurrency in exchange for goods and services

Secure

The Block-chain network keeps all your data and transactions private giving you absolute security
